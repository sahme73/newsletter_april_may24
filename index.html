Muqami Newsletter — April/May 2024

Words of Wisdom


During his sermon on March 24, 1928, Hazrat Musleh-e-Maud(ra) said:


“It is hope and certainty that bring a true Eid, and it is this very hope and certainty that was brought by the Promised Messiah(as). […] There is a sense of discomfort and worry amongst all nations, communities, and countries. However, if there is any Jama’at that is firm in its Faith and filled with hope and certainty, it is the Ahmadiyya [Muslim] Jama’at. […] We are happy that God Almighty has promised us that no one can ever destroy us.”

April/May Highlights

Eid al-Fitr



Volunteers gathered for a picture with Sadr Sahib who assisted with parking and security on Eid. Muqami Khuddam showcasing fantastic team effort!


Disaster/Emergency Preparedness Program

Alhamdulillah, Maryland Jama’at held a disaster/emergency preparedness program. We were honored to have Respected Naib Ameer Bilal Rana Sahib, National Secretary Umur-e-Amma, join us. Naib Sadr Sahib led the program.

Muqami BBQ


Muqami Majlis held Salat Brotherhood and BBQ at Sira-e-Khidmat followed by Maghrib and Isha Prayers at Baitur Rahman.


MKA MIST


Muqami Khuddam represented the Jama’at at this year’s Masroor International Sports Tournament (MIST), competing fiercely in California.


East/Muqami Interregional Ijtema


An interregional took place between both the East and the Muqami regions at Baagh-e-Ahmad. The Ijtema tightened the bond between Khuddam and included activities such as sports, fireside chats, and more.
